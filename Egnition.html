Egnition

Egnition is a freshers' orientation program tailor made for the freshers of KGEC. This is keeping in line with the traditions, where you are imparted with handy knowledge, necessary to get a keen idea not only about businesses, but also about how stuff works in the corporate world. It starts off typically with various sessions such as TechQuiz, Advertise your Product and open QnA sessions. With these KGEC E Cell aims to dispel the myth that surrounds the startups : the workplace culture, the amount of hard work required and the fact that it is not at all easy money. Moreover even if you are not interested in an MBA or a startup (because frankly it is quite laborious) you can land a lucrative internship or a PPO (Pre placement Offer). There are also inspiring words from guest entrepreneurs who have slogged out in the real word and established themselves. It is a mind blowing experience with inputs on how to survive in this fast paced, incredibly competitive environment. Also, there is the added bonus of a TechQuiz that tests one's knowledge of the business world. However you can also display your management as well as promotional skills in Advertise Your Product which is a practical managerial session. However, even if some doubt persists about how to 'Start-UP' feel free to badger the members of E Cell with questions in the QnA session. So, if you are willing to take the leap of faith then the world of Entrepreneurship is here for you, my friend.
